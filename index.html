 Chris Brown
 Doctoral Candidate, NC State

I am a 5th year Computer Science PhD student at North Carolina State University advised by Dr. Chris Parnin. My research focuses on improving developer behavior and productivity by applying behavioral science concepts, such as nudge theory, to improve recommendations to programmers and increase adoption of useful software engineering tools and practices. Please feel free to contact me if you are interested in discussing my research, proposing new ideas, or collaborating.
